About Me

I began my career as a Software Engineer, and that technical experience continues to shape how I think about systems, delivery, and problem-solving. Over time, I found myself increasingly drawn to the work of clarifying requirements, aligning stakeholders, and helping teams move forward with purpose.

That is why I’m now intentionally transitioning toward business systems analysis and agile management. My Scrum Master and team lead experience, especially in requirements-focused work, has strengthened my ability to bridge business needs and technical execution without losing the engineering perspective I built my career on.

I also leverage modern AI tools such as OpenAI technologies, GitHub Copilot, and Anthropic Claude to enhance productivity, accelerate development workflows, and improve decision-making across analysis and delivery processes.

My focus is on growing into roles where I can contribute through analysis, facilitation, and delivery leadership while still drawing on my software engineering background to support better decisions and stronger outcomes.
